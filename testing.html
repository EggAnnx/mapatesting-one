Concpet #2

One way to express oneself is through fashion. You want to make a statement about who you are and how you want other people to see you. Individualism, authenticity, and fashion are important. Although we took current trends into consideration when designing, we wanted to emphasize uniqueness outside what is trending. Because we also have a tendency to stick to conservative values. So this outfit invites you to think beyond the box! The concept was to combine layers, with pants layered at the bottom and a skirt that falls to create a lovely silhouette. It's also inspired by wrap-around pants/skirts that have gained popularity, it's a way to keep the silhouette simple yet sophisticated. The design, like the third concept, was made with discard/scrap fabricsand follow a scrappy design through texture but follow a more simple and elevated silhouette.
