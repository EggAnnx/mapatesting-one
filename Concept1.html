Concpet #1

❮ ❯

It’s a simple jacket with an oversized exaggerated collar aspects that are both well-known and unusual to provide a striking look. The oversized collar of the jacket provides a striking detail that draws the eye to the neckline and frames the face. The zigzag designs on the jacket were inspired by tattoo designs and traditional fabrics from the Philippines. The rich cultural legacy of the nation is reflected in these patterns. Through the use of these symbols, the jacket honors the regional creative traditions and gives the design a hint of cultural value. The jacket gains visual interest and a dynamic aspect from the zigzag patterns, which include strong and angular lines. It presents a new perspective on fashion that is both cutting edge and deeply entrenched in history by fusing current design with cultural inspirations.
